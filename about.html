About TMG College of Arts & Science

Founded in 2000 and affiliated to the University of Madras, TMG College of Arts & Science is a private, co-educational institution committed to academic excellence, ethical leadership, and holistic student development.

Our Heritage & Vision

Established by Chairman Dr. T. M. Gunaraja, the college has grown steadily over two decades to become a respected centre of higher learning, emphasizing inquiry, skill development, and character formation.

Academic Environment

TMGCAS offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Commerce, Science, Computer Applications, and Management with experienced faculty and strong academic culture.

Comprehensive Programs

UG and PG degrees across commerce, science, IT, and management.

Prime Location

Conveniently located near Tambaram with easy access.

Infrastructure

Modern classrooms, labs, library, and sports facilities.

Student Development

Focus on leadership, communication, and employability skills.
